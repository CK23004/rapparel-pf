Shipping Policy

At Rapparel Solutions Pvt. Ltd., we strive to provide a seamless and reliable shopping experience, ensuring that your favorite branded lifestyle products reach you quickly and in perfect condition. Below are the details of our shipping policies:

1. Delivery Coverage

  • We deliver products sourced directly from branded stores to addresses within our serviceable locations.
  • Delivery locations are based on our operational zones and are subject to change as we expand our services.

2. Delivery Timeframes

Hyperlocal Deliveries: Orders will be delivered within 90 minutes of order confirmation, subject to product availability and distance from the store.

3. Shipping Charges

  • Shipping charges (if applicable) will be displayed at checkout before the order is placed.
  • Free delivery may be offered as part of promotional campaigns or for orders exceeding a specific value.

4. Order Tracking

  • Customers can track their orders through the tracking link shared via email/SMS or directly on the Rapparel web app under the "My Orders" section.
  • Updates regarding the delivery status will be shared in real-time.

5. Undeliverable Orders

  • If an order is undeliverable due to incorrect address details provided by the customer, the order will be canceled.
  • In such cases, a refund will be initiated after deducting applicable shipping charges.

6. Failed Delivery Attempts

Re-delivery requests can be raised through customer support, subject to additional charges.

7. Damaged or Missing Items

  • If a product is damaged or an item is missing from the delivery package, customers must report it to Rapparel's customer support within 24 hours of delivery.
  • Resolutions include replacement or refund, subject to verification and the terms of the return policy.

8. Delivery Exceptions

Deliveries may be delayed due to unforeseen circumstances such as extreme weather, natural disasters, or operational challenges. In such cases, we will keep customers informed and work to resolve the situation as quickly as possible.

9. Contact Information

For any queries or assistance with shipping, please reach out to our customer support team at info@Rapparelsolutions.com or call 9220492962.

We are committed to ensuring a smooth and enjoyable shopping experience with Rapparel. Your trust and satisfaction are our top priorities.
