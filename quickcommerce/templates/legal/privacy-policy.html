Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 17th December 2024

At Rapparel Solutions Private Limited ("Company"), we are deeply committed to safeguarding your personal information's privacy and security. Your trust is of paramount importance to us, and protecting your privacy remains our top priority.

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, process, and disclose your information. By accessing and using our website/app/platform and affiliated services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy (“Privacy Policy”) in addition to our ‘Terms of Use.’ We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we collect, use, and disclose your information, as well as to stay updated on any changes or updates we make to this policy.

This Privacy Policy applies to all websites, products, and services linked to it. However, it does not apply to affiliates and partners that have their own privacy policies. In such cases, we recommend reviewing the privacy policy on the relevant site.

If you have any questions or need clarification regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@Rapparelsolutions.com.

Understanding the Coverage of This Policy

Rapparel Solutions Private Limited ("Rapparel," "the Company," "we," "us," and "our") values your privacy and is committed to safeguarding it. This policy outlines:

  • The types of information that Rapparel may collect directly from you or through automated means when you access or use its website, application, and other online services (collectively referred to as "Services"); and
  • Our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

This policy applies only to the information collected by Rapparel through its Services, including emails, text, and other electronic communications sent through or related to the Services. It does not cover information that you provide to or is collected by any third parties you use in connection with the Services. Rapparel encourages you to consult the privacy policies of those third parties directly.

Please read this policy carefully to understand Rapparel's practices regarding your information and how we will treat it. By accessing or using our Services and/or registering for an account with Rapparel, you agree to this privacy policy and consent to Rapparel's collection, use, disclosure, retention, and protection of your personal information as outlined here. If you do not provide the required information, Rapparel may be unable to provide you with all of its Services.

This policy may be updated from time to time, and your continued use of Rapparel's Services after any changes will be deemed acceptance of those changes. Therefore, please check this policy periodically for updates.

Permissible Age

The Services are not intended for users under the age of 18, unless permitted by applicable local laws (“Permissible Age”). We do not knowingly collect personal information from users under the Permissible Age, nor do we market to or solicit information from anyone under the Permissible Age.

Data Collection and Utilization

Rapparel collects different types of information from users of our Services, including:

  • Personal Information: This refers to data that can be associated with a specific individual and can be used to identify that individual either on its own or when combined with other information we possess.
  • Non-Personal Information: This includes data such as your internet connection details, the device(s) you use to access our Services, usage details, and other information that is not related to your identity.

We collect this information in various ways: directly from you when you provide it; indirectly from third parties (such as social media sites authorized by you); and automatically as you use our Services (e.g., usage details, IP addresses, cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies).

1. User-Provided Data

The information we collect through our Services may include:

  • Personal Information: Name, address, email address, postal code, password, and other details you may provide when creating your account, such as gender, mobile number, date of birth, anniversary date, user bio, and website.
  • Your Content: Information you submit through our Services, such as order details, history, contacts for various features, and other details you provide on our Services or account profile. You may provide contacts (e.g., friends or others) to avail certain Services, and by doing so, you confirm that you have consent from those individuals to share their personal information with Rapparel.
  • Your Searches and Other Activities: Search terms you have looked up and results you selected.
  • Your Browsing Information: Data on how long you use our Services, which features you access, and the ads you click on.
  • Your Communications: Messages between you and delivery partners or sellers, some of which may be recorded for internal purposes; participation in surveys, polls, sweepstakes, contests, or promotions; requests for features like newsletters, updates, or products; interactions with customer service and grievance redressal mechanisms. If you send information from the Services via SMS, we may log your phone number, carrier, and the date and time of the message.
  • Your Transactional Information: If you make purchases through our Services, we collect and store information to process your requests and complete forms for future transactions, such as mobile number, address, email, billing, and payment details. This information may be shared with third parties, such as PCI-compliant payment processors. Credit card information is encrypted using industry-standard technologies.
  • Image/Video Data: We may collect and process images and video footage of individuals attending live events or visiting our office/store for security purposes. This includes CCTV footage capturing individuals' images, vehicles, and other details. We may also collect photos or videos during events for promotional or documentation purposes, which may be shared with business or event partners.

2. Information We Collect Through Automatic Data Collection Technologies

We may automatically collect certain information about your devices (including mobile devices) and your use of our Services, even if you haven’t registered or logged in.

  • Usage Information: Data about your use of our Services, including traffic data, location data, logs, communication data, and the resources you access and use through our Services.
  • Computer and Device Information: Information about your device, including IP address, operating system, platform, browser type, and other browsing details like connection speed and type, device type, device identifiers, mobile network info, and your device's phone number.
  • Stored Information and Files: Our apps may access metadata and other information associated with files stored on your mobile device, such as photos, audio and video clips, contacts, and address book info.
  • Location Information: We collect real-time location data from your device, as permitted by you.
  • Last URL Visited: The URL of the last web page you visited before visiting our websites.
  • Mobile Device IDs: When using our Services on a mobile device, we may use mobile device identifiers (e.g., IDFA) to recognize you and store your preferences, and track your usage. Unlike cookies, mobile device IDs are not deletable and may be used by advertising and analytics companies to track app usage and show relevant ads.
  • Your Preferences: Information about your preferences, such as time zone and language settings.
  • Your Activity on the Services: Data about your activities on the Services, including search queries, comments, domain names, search results selected, clicks, pages viewed, session duration, error logs, and other similar details.
  • Mobile Status: For mobile users, the online/offline status of your application.
  • Applications: Information about the presence and details of other apps on your mobile device. This data helps us understand your preferences and provide a more personalized experience.
  • Precise Location Information: For location-based services, we collect GPS data from your device, including latitude, longitude, or altitude, and the time of the data capture. This information is used to customize location-based features.
  • Cookies and Other Electronic Tools: Rapparel and its third-party partners may use cookies, pixel tags, web beacons, device IDs, and other similar technologies to collect information regarding your use of our Services and third-party websites. These tools help us enhance your experience, deliver personalized content and ads, perform analytics, track usage, and assist with security and administrative functions.

3. Data from External Sources

We may collect, process, and store your user ID linked to any social media accounts (such as Facebook or Google) that you use to sign into the Services or interact with them. By connecting your social media account with Rapparel, you consent to the collection, storage, and use of the information shared through that social media interface in accordance with this Privacy Policy. This may include public information from your social media profile, information shared with us by the social media service, or details provided during the sign-in process. Please refer to the privacy policies of your social media providers for more information on how they share data.

Additionally, we may obtain information about you from third-party partners, marketers, websites, and researchers and combine that data with information we already have about you.
