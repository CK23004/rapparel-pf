Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will my order be delivered?

Rapparel aims to deliver orders within 90 minutes of purchase. However, delivery times may vary based on distance, location, and other factors.

How does Rapparel ensure product authenticity?

We collaborate directly with verified brand stores to guarantee that all products are 100% authentic.

Where does Rapparel deliver?

Currently, our services are available in Gurgaon, with plans to expand to additional cities soon.

What is Rapparel’s return window?

Products can be returned within 24 hours of delivery. Please note that some items may be non-returnable due to brand-specific policies, which are detailed on the product page or at checkout.

What happens if there’s a delay in delivery?

In the rare event of a delay, you can contact our support team for prompt assistance and resolution.

Can I cancel my order after placing it?

Orders cannot be canceled once they have been placed.

How does Rapparel handle refunds?

Refunds are processed within seven working days upon receiving approval for the returned product from the brand.

How can I track my order?

Real-time tracking details are shared through the web app once your order is placed.

Can I place multiple orders?

Yes, multiple orders can be placed, but each will be processed and delivered individually.

How can I give feedback about Rapparel?

Feedback is always welcome and can be shared via our contact number or email. Your input helps us enhance our services.
