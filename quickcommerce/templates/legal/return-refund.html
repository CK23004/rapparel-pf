Return and Refunds Policy

Conditions for Return

You may return a product from an order under the following conditions:

  • If the wrong item was delivered, i.e., different from what you ordered, or
  • If the item was substantially damaged or deteriorated in quality at the time of delivery.

You agree to provide all necessary proof, including but not limited to images of the product with issues.

Initiating a Return or Exchange

You must inspect the products upon delivery and initiate an exchange or return with the required proof via the Platform. You may also contact our customer care to initiate an exchange or return.

IT IS CLARIFIED THAT EXCHANGE OR RETURNS WILL NOT BE ACCEPTED IF THE PRODUCT DOES NOT HAVE THE RAPPAREL “SEAL TAG” OR IF THE PRODUCT IS USED IN PART OR FULL.

Eligibility for Return or Exchange

You may request an exchange or return for products purchased from us, provided:

  • The products have the Rapparel seal tag.
  • The products are unused and in their original condition.
  • The request is made within 24 hours of delivery.

Requests for exchanges or returns will not be accepted after 24 hours from the time of delivery.

Refund Processing

If refund is approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 7-10 business days.

Unless otherwise stated or mutually agreed upon between Rapparel and the customer:

  • Refunds for payments made via electronic methods (such as debit card, credit card, net banking, UPI, etc.) will be processed in the same manner as the original payment or provided in the form of credit/cashback/coupons/promotional codes.
  • Refunds for cash on delivery purchases will be provided in the form of promotional codes, which will expire 30 days from the date of issuance.
  • Promotional codes issued for refunds will expire 7 days from their issuance unless otherwise stated.

Refunds cannot be transferred back to any other payment method once the credit/cashback/coupon/promotional code is issued.

Currency for Refunds

All refunds will be made in Indian Rupees only.
